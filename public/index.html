What is Study Journal?

Study Journal is a comprehensive web application designed to help students and learners track their educational progress, test their knowledge, and build better study habits.

Key Features

  • Study Log: Keep detailed records of your study sessions
  • Multiple Choice Quizzes: Test your understanding
  • Progress Tracking: View your study history
  • Interactive Charts: Visualize your study patterns
  • Achievement System: Unlock badges and rewards
  • Study Streaks: Track consecutive days of studying
  • Search Function: Find specific journal entries
  • Theme Customization: Personalize your experience

How It Works

1. Create Study Entries: Record what you studied and your reflections.

2. Take Quizzes: Test your knowledge with AI-generated questions.

3. Review Progress: Check your statistics and charts.

4. Unlock Achievements: Reach milestones and earn badges.

5. Build Streaks: Study consistently every day.
